Musnad Aḥmad

Compiled by Imam Aḥmad ibn Ḥanbal (d. 855 CE), founder of the Ḥanbalī school. Organized by companion (musnad-style) rather than by topic. One of the largest hadith compilations — approximately 27,000–30,000 reports across roughly 900 companions.
