Musnad al-Shāfiʿī

Compiled from the narrations of Imam Muḥammad ibn Idrīs al-Shāfiʿī (d. 820 CE), founder of the Shāfiʿī school. Approximately 1,800 hadiths, primarily of legal-jurisprudential interest.

Translation note: No complete English translation is available for free distribution. The Arabic text is readily available on archive.org; partial translations exist in academic works.
