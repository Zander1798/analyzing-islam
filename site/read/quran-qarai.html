The Qurʾān — Phrase-by-Phrase

Ali Quli Qarai's English rendering of the Qurʾān presents each Arabic phrase immediately followed by its English equivalent, so the reader can trace the translation choice phrase by phrase. Useful for comparing against the Saheeh International edition and for seeing where translators insert parenthetical smoothings.
