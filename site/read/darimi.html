Sunan al-Dārimī

Compiled by 'Abd Allāh al-Dārimī (d. 869 CE). Arranged thematically like a sunan, covering jurisprudence, worship, commerce, and the virtues of the Qurʾan. Approximately 3,400 hadiths across 23 books. Widely cited in classical Sunnī scholarship despite not being part of the canonical six.
