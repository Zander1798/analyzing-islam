Musnad Aḥmad ibn Ḥanbal

Compiled by Imam Aḥmad ibn Ḥanbal (d. 855 CE), founder of the Ḥanbalī school of jurisprudence. Organized by companion (musnad-style) rather than thematically. One of the largest extant hadith compilations — approximately 27,000 reports transmitted through roughly 900 companions, including many not preserved in the canonical six.
