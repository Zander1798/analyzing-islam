Jāmi al-Tirmidhī

Compiled by al-Tirmidhī (d. 892 CE). Notable for the compiler's own grading (ṣaḥīḥ, ḥasan, ḍaʿīf) of each hadith. About 3,950 reports in total. This is the complete all-in-one English edition.
