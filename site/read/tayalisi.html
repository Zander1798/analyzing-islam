Musnad al-Ṭayālisī

Compiled by Sulaymān ibn Dāwūd al-Ṭayālisī (d. 819 CE). One of the oldest musnad-style hadith compilations, preceding the canonical six. Approximately 2,900 hadiths organised by companion.

Translation note: No complete English translation available. Arabic text on archive.org, with Urdu translation published by traditional Indian subcontinent publishers.
