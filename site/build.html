Build

Compose your own arguments inside a side-by-side workspace. Quote every Qur'an verse, every hadith, every catalog entry — drag it straight into a rich-text editor, edit and style it, then save and share.

How this works

1

Open the builder

Hit + Create to open an empty workspace: a full rich-text editor on the left, a filterable source browser on the right.

2

Pull in sources

Pick any readable source — Qur'an, the six hadith collections, Tanakh, New Testament, Mishnah, Talmud, Ibn Kathīr, or any catalog view — then search for a verse or keyword. Highlight what you want and drag it into the editor.

3

Argue in your own voice

Style your prose with fonts, headings, colour, bold, italics, highlights. Dropped quotations are fully editable — edit, restyle, or translate non-English words (Hebrew, Greek, Arabic) in-place.

4

Save and share

Save the build under a name of your choice. Come back later to edit, or generate a share link anyone can read.
